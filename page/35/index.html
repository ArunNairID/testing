MySQL: Free Software but not Open Source

Wed, May 14, 2008 The title of MySQL’s website states that they are the world’s most popular open-source database. This is false; MySQL is not an open-source database. That assertion is a fact, not an opinion. MySQL is Free Software, licensed under the GNU GPL. People frequently use the two phrases “Free Software” and “Open Source Software” as synonyms, but there are very large, very important differences. The difference between Free and Open Source Open Source is much more of a development methodology than a philosophical standpoint.
