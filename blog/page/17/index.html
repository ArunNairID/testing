How to gather statistics at regular intervals

Fri, Mar 18, 2011 I gather a lot of statistics such as performance data. Sometimes I have multiple things going on a system and I want to be able to align and compare the resulting data from multiple processes later. That means they need to be aligned on time intervals. Here is a naive way to gather stats at intervals: while sleep 1; do gather-some-stats; done There are two problems: each iteration will take longer than a second, so there will be drift; and the iterations will not be aligned exactly on the clock ticks, so the data isn’t as easy to correlate with other samples.
